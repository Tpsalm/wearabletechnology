About Wearable Tech Hub

We started in 2023 to answer one question: how do these devices actually perform day-to-day?

No lab coats. No medical claims. We buy every device at retail price and test them for two weeks minimum. We track battery drain, comfort during sleep, app responsiveness, and feature accuracy as described by the manufacturer.

Our team consists of consumer tech reviewers, not medical professionals. We clearly label when a feature is FDA-cleared (if officially stated by the manufacturer) and link to the source.
